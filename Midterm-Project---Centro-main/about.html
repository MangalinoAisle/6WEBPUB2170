Our Story

Centro Café opened in November 2024 with a mission to create a cozy corner for coffee lovers and food enthusiasts. Founded by Karen and Glenn Mercado, we are located in the Sto. Rosario Heritage District, Angeles City, Pampanga. We serve memorable drinks and hearty meals inspired by Kapampangan flavors, all in a warm and welcoming environment. More than just a café, Centro Café is a community hub where friends, students, and families gather. Here, coffee, comfort, and community come together in the heart of Angeles City.
